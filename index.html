ABOUT ME

Visual Storyteller & Operational Partner

My digital journey started in 2015, running my own online thrift shop. Managing product listings, customer inquiries, and daily sales taught me the foundations of e-commerce and organization. Since then, I've grown brand presence across Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok for ministries and local businesses — with 4+ years of hands-on experience.

Most recently, I managed social media for Roofing Center, where I planned weekly content, designed Reels and graphics, and used analytics to refine posting strategies that drove sales inquiries. For over six years, I also helped Agape Reaching People Ministry maintain a consistent, engaging online presence with zero budget — proving that strategy matters more than spending.

As an affiliate content creator, I write product-focused captions, generate affiliate links across TikTok, Facebook, and Instagram, and track performance to identify top-converting products — driving consistent click-through and sales.

Let's grow together — consistently, visibly, and without the overwhelm.

I'm certified in Short-Form Content Mastery, Social Media Marketing, and Data Analytics (2025) — so my content decisions are always data-informed, not guesswork.

Beyond content, I bring strong backend support: data entry, file organization, email management, and accounts payable experience (processing invoices with zero errors using Excel and Jenzabar).

Whether you need a content calendar, engaging Reels, affiliate-driven posts, analytics reports, or someone to handle daily digital tasks — I'm here to work quietly behind the scenes so your brand stays active and you stay focused.
